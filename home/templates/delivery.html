Delivery

UK Delivery


All our UK deliveries are simply charged at a flat rate of £4 using a Signed For/Tracked service. The cost of delivery will be included when you add an product to your cart. If you have any queries, please just contact us through the form and we’ll try our very best to accommodate your request.

Standard Delivery /Timescales


We usually ship all orders next day but guarantee shipping within 2-5 working days from when the order is received. Once dispatched, we will email you to let you know the goods have been sent.

European and International Delivery


Unfortunately we do not yet deliver to any European or International customers. If you are however interested in our products and are from outside the UK. Please do get in touch. We hope to expand to a wider audience once we find out feet.

Returns & Exchanges: Damaged goods


In the unlikely event that your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately so that we can arrange a replacement. For insurance purposes, we usually need to have some sort of photographic evidence of the damage and will also require the damaged goods and original packaging to be returned to us before we can ship a replacement to you.
